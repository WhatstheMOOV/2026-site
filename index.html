Frequently asked

MOOV integrates with PowerSchool, eSchoolData, and more. For eSchoolData and PowerSchool, present/tardy statuses are sent in real-time, while absences are set at the end of the period. For all other SIS systems, data is synced at the end of the day.

Staff visit staff.joinmoov.com and students visit student.joinmoov.com. Both can sign in with their school Google or Microsoft account — no extra passwords needed.

The Magic Reader is MOOV's tap-to-scan device. Students simply tap their school ID card on the reader to check in. It automates attendance, saves teachers 2 minutes per period, and syncs everything to your SIS in real-time.

MOOV can be set up in minutes, not months. Our team handles the onboarding, SIS connection, and device setup. Most schools are live within a week.
