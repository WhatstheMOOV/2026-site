The Team

Meet the people
behind MOOV

Kevin

CEO

Kevin walked into his high school every single day completely unaccounted for. No one verified he entered the building. Attendance was taken by hand—if it was taken at all. Hall passes were literal blocks of wood. And everyone just accepted it.

But Kevin never accepted it—and this wasn't the first time. In high school, he founded a club called the Student Pipeline, created to do something no one in his district had done before: give students a direct line to the Board of Education. Kevin became the district's first-ever student liaison to the board—bridging the communication gap between students and administrators, delivering real feedback, and fighting to improve the student experience from the inside. It taught him something crucial: the systems meant to serve students were rarely built with students in mind.

That realization never left him. So when he looked around and saw schools that couldn't answer the most basic safety question—who is actually in this building right now?—he didn't wait for someone else to fix it. He built MOOV. What started as a vision born out of firsthand frustration has grown into a system now serving some of the smallest and largest schools in the country—featured on FOX and sweeping across districts nationwide. But the road wasn't easy. Kevin built MOOV from scratch—writing firmware, wiring hardware, pitching skeptical administrators, and proving that a young founder could solve a problem the education system had ignored for decades.

Kevin was also a walk-on to the Notre Dame Men's Rowing Team and a United States Marine—two commitments that taught him what it means to have grit, stay disciplined, and be a part of something larger than himself.

Samera

Director of Operations

Samera is a recent(…ish) public schooler with a history of chronic tardiness. There was no motivation for her to change her behavior, and post-high school, she envisioned how to make the school experience better for students and staff alike.
