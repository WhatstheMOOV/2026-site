The Team

Meet the people
behind MOOV

Kevin

CEO

Kevin is a recent(...ish) public schooler who would walk into his school unaccounted for. With no verification at entry, manual attendance in the classroom, and blocks of wood for hall passes, he knew there had to be a better system for accountability and attendance. After launching MOOV at his former high school, the system has swept across the country, been featured on FOX, and is now helping some of the smallest and largest schools in the U.S. become safer and more streamlined. Kevin is also a walk-on to the Notre Dame Men's Rowing Team and a US Marine.

Samera

Director of Operations

Samera is a recent(...ish) public schooler with a history of chronic tardiness. There was no motivation for her to change her behavior, and post-high school, she envisioned how to make the school experience better for students and staff alike.

Ashley

Product Development

Ashley is a recent(...ish) public schooler turned public educator. As an AP Biology teacher and aspiring administrator at Babylon Junior-Senior High School, Ashley had experienced the problems MOOV solves from an educator's perspective. She has helped develop the product from experiences in her own classroom, directly inspiring features such as hall pass counts, administrative reports and dashboards, and more.
