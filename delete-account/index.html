Delete Your Account

How to permanently delete your MOOV account and associated data

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your MOOV account directly from the app. To do so:

  1. Open the MOOV app on your device
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted

What Happens When You Delete Your Account

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  1. Your profile information and credentials
  2. Your activity history and session data
  3. Any personalized settings or preferences

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. If you wish to use MOOV again after deleting your account, you will need to create a new account.

Need Help?

If you are unable to access the app or need assistance deleting your account, please contact us at support@joinmoov.com and we will process your request.
